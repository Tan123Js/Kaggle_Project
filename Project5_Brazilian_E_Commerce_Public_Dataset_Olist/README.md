**Project Title**

**Brazilian E-Commerce Analysis**

**Project Context**

This project analyzed a Brazilian E-Commerce dataset encompassing 119,143 orders across 39 variables, focusing on marketplace growth, customer behavior, operational performance, and seller dynamics. The analysis aimed to uncover key business challenges and actionable strategies for sustainable growth.

**Core Problem**

The platform suffers from extremely low customer retention, with a repeat purchase rate of only 3.12%. Approximately 90% of customers make a single purchase, indicating heavy reliance on acquiring new customers rather than maintaining long-term relationships.

**Key Findings**:

**1.Customer Retention Deficit**

- Repeat purchase rate: 3.12% (benchmark: 20–40% for typical e-commerce platforms)
- Most customers purchase once; platform lacks loyalty mechanisms

**2.Delivery Performance Impacts Customer Satisfaction**

- On-time deliveries average review score: 4.13
- Late deliveries average review score: 2.44
- Delivery delays strongly affect customer satisfaction

**3.Revenue Concentration Among High-Value Customers**

- Customer revenue highly skewed: small portion contributes majority of revenue
- Targeted strategies can maximize revenue from high-value segments

**4.Marketplace Activity Concentrated Among Few Sellers**

- Top seller: 1,854 orders; top revenue seller: 258,882 total sales
- Dependence on a limited number of sellers introduces operational risk

**5.Operational Insights**

- Product categories such as health_beauty and bed_bath_table dominate orders and revenue
- Delivery logistics: average delivery time ~12 days; ~7.11% late deliveries

**Business Implications & Recommendations**:

**1.Implement Membership & Loyalty Programs**

- Introduce tiered membership with rewards points, exclusive deals, or purchase-based benefits
- Encourage repeat purchases and strengthen customer lifetime value

**2.Enhance Delivery & Logistics Performance**

- Monitor seller shipping reliability and optimize logistics
- Reduce late deliveries to improve customer satisfaction scores

**3.Target High-Value Customers**

- Identify top-spending customers and provide personalized offers
- Increase retention and maximize long-term revenue potential

**4.Diversify and Support Seller Ecosystem**

- Reduce dependency on top sellers by helping smaller sellers scale
- Implement seller performance monitoring and support programs

**Strategic Takeaway**

Focusing on customer retention as the core problem, while simultaneously improving logistics, leveraging high-value customers, and strengthening the seller ecosystem, can drive sustainable marketplace growth, improve customer satisfaction, and maximize long-term profitability.
